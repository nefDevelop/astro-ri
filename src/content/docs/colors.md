## Seed Color

The entire color system derives from a single seed color.

## Harmony Types

- **tonal**: Subtle variations of the same hue
- **analogous**: Adjacent hues on the color wheel
- **triadic**: Three evenly spaced hues
- **complementary**: Opposite hues
